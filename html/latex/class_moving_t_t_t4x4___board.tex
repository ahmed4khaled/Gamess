\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Implements a 4x4 Moving Tic-\/\+Tac-\/\+Toe board. 

In this variant, pieces can be moved after the board becomes full. The board inherits from \doxylink{class_board}{Board$<$char$>$} and overrides all required logic.
