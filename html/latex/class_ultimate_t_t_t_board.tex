\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Implements the logic for Ultimate Tic-\/\+Tac-\/\+Toe. 

The board consists of\+:
\begin{DoxyItemize}
\item A 9×9 grid.
\item Divided into 3×3 sub-\/boards (each 3×3).
\item Players win a sub-\/board using normal XO rules.
\item Winning sub-\/boards populate a 3×3 "{}main board"{}.
\item Winning the main board (3-\/in-\/a-\/row of sub-\/boards) wins the game.
\end{DoxyItemize}

Also enforces “forced board rules”\+:
\begin{DoxyItemize}
\item Your opponent\textquotesingle{}s move determines the sub-\/board you must play in next.
\item If that sub-\/board is closed (won/tied), you may play anywhere legal. 
\end{DoxyItemize}
